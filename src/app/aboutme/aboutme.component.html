Software Developer

I am a software developer who is very passionate about creating products that solve real world problems. I am well rounded, internally movitated , results driven, and goal oriented. I thrive working in a dynamic team environment that promotes growth, teamwork, and development.

I’ve always had a drive to create new things. Innovation lives in my blood. From a very young age I always tinkered with new technology, sometimes breaking them to find out how it works and putting them back together. Needless to say this became an expensive hobby, but that curiosity never left me, It is part of who I am.

I got into coding as an off shoot from my creativity. Integrating design, User Interfaces, and code into a working product that others can use drives me everyday. I love the feeling of delivering a finish product to a client. Let's work on something today!
